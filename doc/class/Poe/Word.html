Class: Poe.Word

Defined in: coffee/Word.coffee
Inherits: Poe.TextObject

Overview

Poe.Word is the base of all text in the document. With no formatting in the document and only text, each Poe.Word contains one text word followed by a space. However once formatting comes in words are split and that is not always the case.

Instance Method Summary
